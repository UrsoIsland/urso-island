Urso Island Game Rules

Last updated: May, 31st, 2020.

There are a few rules you must follow when playing Urso Island:

  1. Do not insult, threaten, harass, or flame other players. This includes racism, sexism and homophobic behaviour.
  2. Be respectful to all the other players.
  3. Do not post links to illegal or dangerous websites.
  4. Do not share or ask for personal or sensitive data. This includes phone number, address, nationality and real name.
